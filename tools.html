Learning Tools and Resources

Below is a comprehensive list of available online learning tools such as dictionaries, lessons, speech synthesizers, and various media that are useful for learning Irish.

Dictionaries / Aids
Focail.ie An online Irish-English dictionary.
Pota Focail An online Irish-English dictionary that draws from a number of sources. Gives examples of useage.
An Focloir Beag An online Irish verb conjugation dictionary. It is written entirely in Irish, so you will have to know the Irish for different verb forms in order to figure it out. You can use a dictionary to get you started, and work from there.
Abair.ie An online text-to-speach synthesizer.
Focloir Gaeilge-Béarla Commonly shortened to 'FGB', this is the definitive Irish-to-English dictionary by Niaill Ó Dhonaill.
English-Irish Dictionary The English-to-Irish companion to Ó Dhonaill's FGB.

Learning
Daltaí na Gaeilge An organization dedicated to promoting the Irish language in the United States. There are class and event listings throughout the states, a forum, and an impressive list of different Seanfhocail (proverbs)
Irish Learners Forum A forum for Learners and Speakers of all levels. A strong and knowledgeable community helps answer any questions and provide guidance.
Nualeargais An extremely comprehensive grammar guide.
Tara's Web Irish A basic introduction to Irish, free.

Media
TG4 Irish language television. You can stream programmes for free. There are all sorts; childrens' programs, game shows, dramas, and some excellent documentaries.
Raidió na Gaeltachta Irish language radio. You can stream this live for free over the internet, using a few different apps, or download podcasts to play on-the-go.
